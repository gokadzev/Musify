Musify

The open-source music app for Android that offers streaming and offline listening.
Enjoy custom playlists, high-quality audio, and lyrics support - all completely free, with no ads or subscriptions.

Notice

Musify does not have any other official websites or domains.
It is recommended to download the builds either from here, GitHub, or F-Droid.


App development is currently slowed down due to API-side changes.
Once youtube_explode_dart gets fixes, they will be integrated. In the meantime, you may experience severe lagging and streaming issues.
