Musify

The open-source music app for Android that offers streaming and offline listening.
Enjoy custom playlists, high-quality audio, and lyrics support - all completely free, with no ads or subscriptions.

Notice

Musify does not have any other official websites or domains.
It is recommended to download the builds either from the official website, GitHub, or F-Droid.
